About This Project

Empowering safer roads through AI-powered distraction detection.

What is Distracted Driver Detection?

This project leverages deep learning to identify and classify driver distractions from images. Our goal is to promote road safety by alerting or informing about potentially dangerous behaviors behind the wheel‚Äîsuch as texting, drinking, or reaching behind while driving.

How It Works

Image Input: Users upload images of a driver captured inside a vehicle.
Model Prediction: A VGG16-based Convolutional Neural Network processes the image to detect behavior.
Results Display: Users get the predicted behavior with a confidence chart to help visualize model certainty.

Our Mission

The mission of the Distracted Driver Detection project is to increase awareness and provide accessible tools to analyze driving behavior using AI. Whether you're a road safety researcher, developer, educator, or simply a concerned driver, this tool can contribute to safer roads and help curb the rising problem of distracted driving.
