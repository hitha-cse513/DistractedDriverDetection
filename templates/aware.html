The Dangers of Distracted Driving

Distracted driving is one of the leading causes of road accidents worldwide. A moment of looking away from the road ‚Äî whether it's texting, eating, or adjusting the radio ‚Äî can lead to life-changing consequences.

The purpose of this system is not just to detect distractions, but to educate and spread awareness about how we can all contribute to safer roads.

Tips to Stay Focused

Put your phone away: Use hands-free options or turn off notifications while driving.

Avoid eating or drinking: Eat before driving or take breaks if you're on a long trip.

Manage passengers: Keep conversations light and brief, especially when navigating tough roads.

Pre-set your entertainment: Set your playlist, podcast, or navigation before you start driving.

Don't drive tired: Fatigue is as dangerous as distraction. Rest well before driving.
